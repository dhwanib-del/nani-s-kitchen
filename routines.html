Step by step

Skincare Rituals

A slow guide to mornings, nights, and Sundays — the way Nani kept it calm and simple.

Every morning

Morning Ritual

Three unhurried steps to wake the skin — water, a gentle wash, and light moisture.

  1. 1

    Cleanse

    Start simple: lukewarm water, a little Rose & Tulsi Face Wash, half a minute of circles, then rinse.

    Nani's note: end with cool water to calm things down.
  2. 2

    Rose water press

    While skin is still damp, press pure rose water in with your palms. No cotton, no fuss, just scent and balance.

    Nani's note: keep the bottle in the fridge when it's hot.
  3. 3

    Day moisture

    Work a pea-sized amount of Rosehip Day Cream upward until it disappears. Sunscreen goes on right after.

    Nani's note: upward strokes, never rushed.

Nani never used more than three steps in the morning.

Every evening

Night Ritual

A slow unwind to clear the day away and feed skin overnight.

  1. 1

    Double cleanse

    Melt makeup and sunscreen with a little coconut oil, rinse, then use Neem & Aloe Gel Cleanser to finish the job.

  2. 2

    Night cream

    While skin is warm and damp, layer in Turmeric Night Cream. Let the turmeric work while you sleep.

    Nani's note: night is dinner for your skin, so feed it well.
  3. 3

    Face oil (2–3 nights)

    After cream, press two drops of Kumkumadi Facial Oil into your cheeks and forehead. Press, don't rub.

Consistency mattered more to her than which product it was.

Once a week

Weekly Glow Ritual

A Sunday reset Nani never skipped — gentle scrub, a turmeric mask, and deep moisture.

  1. 1

    Start clean

    Begin with your usual cleanser so every step after can do its work.

  2. 2

    Exfoliate with ubtan

    Mix Ubtan Face Scrub with rose water or milk. Leave for five minutes, then massage gently before rinsing.

    Nani's note: if it tingles, add more liquid until it feels kind.
  3. 3

    Turmeric yogurt mask

    Blend half a teaspoon of turmeric with yogurt and honey. Leave it on for 10–15 minutes, then rinse cool for a warm glow.

    Nani's note: use an old towel — turmeric likes to leave a mark.
  4. 4

    Seal with moisture

    Finish with a generous layer of moisturiser. Let it settle before any makeup touches your face.

She called Sundays "reset day" and never skipped it.

Find your fit

Which routine is right for you?

  • Dry Skin

    Focus on hydration. Use night cream twice daily and never skip face oil. Avoid hot water when cleansing.

    Key product: Turmeric Night Cream

  • Oily Skin

    Use the neem cleanser morning and night. Skip heavy creams — Rosehip Day Cream stays light enough. Use oil only once a week.

    Key product: Neem & Aloe Gel Cleanser

  • Combination Skin

    Use different products on different zones if needed. The ubtan and face wash both suit many skin types. Listen to what your skin tells you.

    Key product: Rose & Tulsi Face Wash

  • Sensitive Skin

    Patch test everything first. Avoid ubtan if you are very reactive. Rose water toner is well tolerated by many sensitive skin types.

    Key product: Kumkumadi Facial Oil
